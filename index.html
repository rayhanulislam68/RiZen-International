Warranty Terms & Conditions

📋 Claim Requirements

To claim the warranty, customers must provide:

  • Valid warranty card or purchase receipt
  • Product box bearing the RiZen International sticker

Only manufacturing defects are covered by the warranty.

❌ What the Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Physical damage (cracks, burns, dents, water damage)
  • Damage caused by abuse, carelessness or improper handling.

🔄 Replacement Policy

  • Product replaced no more than once within warranty period
  • Replaced items are NOT covered under a new warranty
  • * Charging Kits: Warranty for charging kits is only applicable to the Adapter; cables are usually not under the warranty Policy.

⚠️ Warning

📱 Do not drop the product
🌡️ Avoid high temperature, short circuit, and humid environments
👶 Keep out of reach of children
